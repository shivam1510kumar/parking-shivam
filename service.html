Products & Services

1. Mechanical Car Parking Systems

We design, manufacture, and install a wide range of space-saving parking systems including: *Stack Parking Systems (2–3 levels)*

Stack Parking System is an economical and simple parking solution for new buildings or existing societies where parking can be doubled.

This is designed to fit on the surface and double the car space by stacking the cars one above the other. The car must be driven off before the upper platform is lowered.

The stack parking system is designed to park cars of different heights as per the height availability at the customer’s location. It is perfect for residential, commercial projects, and parking garages where vertical stacking of cars is desired.

Special Features

Yellow Pipe Barrier

The pipe barrier gives peace of mind to the driver, confirming the vehicle is correctly parked at the end of the pallet.

Cross Beam Vehicle Sensor

Prevents the upper platform from descending if a car is detected below, ensuring user safety even if signaled.

Slow-Motion

Reduces load on the hydraulic motor, enabling smoother pallet operation and longer hydraulic life.

Indication Lamps & Buzzers

Red light and buzzer alert during movement make it visually obvious that the system is in operation.

Tower Parking Systems:

A Tower Parking System is a fully automated, high-density vertical parking solution that optimizes vehicle storage using minimal ground space. Vehicles are lifted vertically by an elevator and then transferred horizontally into storage racks on multiple levels. The entire process is automated, requiring no driver access inside the structure, which enhances safety and efficiency. Cars are retrieved and returned at the ground level through a user-friendly interface. This system is ideal for urban areas, commercial buildings, residential towers, and airports where land is limited. With a scalable capacity ranging from 20 to over 100 vehicles, it offers a compact, space-saving solution. Key benefits include minimal land usage, high security, low maintenance, and touchless operation. Designed for modern infrastructure needs, Tower Parking supports smart city development and green building standards.

  • High speed Geared motors with counter weight and Guides for smooth lift and lowering
  • Turntable on the ground level for getting the car in the Drive out position.
  • Motor- Chain operated Sliding Mechanism .
  • Complete system monitored by PLC for smooth and error free automation.
  • Back up Limit switches provided for each slot for duplex safety
  • Robust Steel structure.
  • Fully automatic integrated operation.
  • Emergency Shut Down switch.
  • Optional Touch Screen/Numerical Keyboard / RFID Swipe card operation Panel.
    • These systems allow vertical and horizontal vehicle storage, significantly increasing parking capacity in limited spaces
